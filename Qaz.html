About FLL

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) Challenge is an international competition organized by FIRST for students aged 9 to 16. It introduces science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) to children through relevant, exciting hands-on learning.

Participants gain real-world problem-solving experience through a guided, global robotics program, helping today's students and teachers build a better future together.
